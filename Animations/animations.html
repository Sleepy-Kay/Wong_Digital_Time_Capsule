Kimi Ni Todoke means "From Me to You" a series that follows a girl named Sawako who has troubles with her highschool life because she's socially awkward and unfornately visually resembles "Sadako" or the japanese verison of "The grudge." Although she struggles she's able to make friends and meet a love interest who likes her almost instantly. This series as sappy and corny as it gets makes me cry every time and makes me SOOO in love with animation. A comedy, romance, and slice of life type of series that holds a special place in my heart like many other animations. This series however made me relate to the main character alot with how socially awkward I used to be. Now I'm just outright annoying and nothing close to what I used to be back in middle school/high school. HAHHAHAHA
